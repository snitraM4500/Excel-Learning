Tips & Tricks

📐 Formula Reminders

  • AVERAGE: =AVERAGE(A1:A5)
  • SUM: =SUM(A1:A5)
  • Multiplication: =A1*B1
  • Division: =A1/B1
  • Percentage: =(A1/B1)*100

️ Conditional Formulas

  • IF: =IF(A1>100, "High", "Low")
  • RANK: =RANK(A1, A$1:A$10)
  • MAX: =MAX(A1:A5) for highest
  • MIN: =MIN(A1:A5) for lowest
  • AND: =IF(AND(A1>18, A1<25), "Yes", "No")
